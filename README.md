# 🧠 StockSense

### Intelligent Supermarket Management System

> Turning everyday retail operations into a **smart, automated, and data-driven experience**

---

## 🌍 About the Project

StockSense is a web-based supermarket management system built to solve real problems faced by small and medium retail stores.

In many stores, inventory is still managed manually or with basic tools, which often leads to stock mismatches, product wastage, and poor planning. StockSense takes a different approach — it connects everything into one intelligent system that works in real time.

From processing a sale to predicting future demand, every step is automated and connected.

---

## 🚀 What Makes StockSense Different?

Instead of just managing data, StockSense actually **understands and reacts to it**.

* It updates inventory instantly after every sale
* It tracks expiry dates and helps reduce wastage
* It learns from past sales to predict future demand
* It even suggests when to restock products

In short, it behaves more like a **smart assistant for retail stores** rather than just software.

---

## 🧩 Key Features

### 🔐 Secure Access

Users can log in securely with different roles like Admin and Cashier, ensuring proper control over the system.

---

### 🛒 Smooth POS Experience

The billing system is designed to be fast and simple. Products can be searched easily, added to a cart, and billed in seconds.
As soon as a sale is completed, the system automatically updates inventory in the background.

---

### 📦 Smart Inventory Management

Stock is maintained in a structured way using batches, which helps in tracking quantities more accurately.
The system also follows **FEFO (First Expiry First Out)** so that items closer to expiry are sold first.

---

### ⏳ Expiry Tracking

StockSense continuously monitors product expiry dates.
It alerts users when items are about to expire and even suggests discount strategies to reduce losses.

---

### 📊 Predictive Analytics

Using past sales data, the system predicts future demand.
This helps store owners avoid situations like running out of stock or over-purchasing.

---

### 🧾 Automatic Purchase Planning

Based on predictions and current stock, StockSense can suggest what needs to be restocked — making inventory planning much easier.

---

### 📈 Admin Insights

The dashboard gives a clear view of what’s happening in the store:

* Sales performance
* Inventory status
* Alerts and warnings
* Demand trends

---

### 🚨 Alerts System

The system actively notifies users about:

* Low stock
* Near-expiry products
* Sudden demand changes

---

## 🔄 How the System Works

StockSense follows a connected workflow where each step naturally leads to the next:

A sale happens → inventory gets updated → expiry is checked → demand is predicted → restocking is suggested

Everything works together behind the scenes without needing manual effort.
